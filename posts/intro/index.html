Intro

Apr 23, 2023
misc

I feel like writing, in a weird way, is a hobby of mine.

That is not entirely true, though. Rather, I love the process of thinking through writing.

I do not expect my writing to be great, at least right from the get go. The fact that English is my second language does not help. Having said that, I wholeheartedly believe that if I am consistent enough, one day I will reach a decent enough level to be content with myself.

I am a computer programmer. Hence, a large portion of posts on the site should be related to the topic of programming.

Believe it or not, I have other interests in my life. However, I am unsure how much I want to share about those. We shall see.
